Internal knowledge assistant

Your documentation, answering questions in seconds.

Many organizations have the knowledge — in manuals, policies, procedures, emails. The problem is nobody knows where to find it. A knowledge assistant indexes all your documents and lets any team member ask questions in natural language and receive precise answers.

  • Internal document indexing
  • Answers grounded only in your content
  • Web interface or Slack/WhatsApp integration
  • Updates when documentation changes

Best for: clinics, law firms, service companies, support teams.

Customer-facing chat agent

Respond, qualify and route. No human needed for the repetitive stuff.

An AI agent on WhatsApp that handles your customers' frequent questions, captures leads, and escalates to a human when needed. Designed with clear rules: what it answers, what it doesn't, when it escalates.

  • WhatsApp Business integration
  • Answers based on your catalog and FAQs
  • Name and contact capture
  • Smart escalation to human agent
  • Configurable business hours

Best for: businesses with high volume of repetitive WhatsApp inquiries.

Workflow automation
